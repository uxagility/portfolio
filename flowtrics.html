Flowtrics

Early in my career I pioneered a technique of embedding interactive HTML protoypes into survey infrastructure to conduct rapidly deployed, at-scale behavioural studies, zeroing in on specific design aspects via the comparative evaluation of user performance on micro-tasks.

While at YouTube, I deployed dozens of Flowtrics studies for a variety of applications, mostly for early designs of novel features for the YouTube App, YouTube Shopping, and uncovering unknown design problems in YouTube Creaor Studio.

This method bridges the gap between arduous, longitudinal data collection from in-person usability studies, and too-little, too-late live experiments on fully engineered builds, and it still has no commercial equivalent today.
